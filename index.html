What You Get

Vocabulary Lists

Names, medical terms, legal jargon, and technical words automatically pulled from your documents. Scored by importance so you prep the terms that matter most. The app learns your preferences over time — the more you use it, the better it gets at flagging what matters to you.

Semantic Search

Ask natural-language questions about your case documents and get answers with source citations. Great for targeted questions across a breadth of documents.

Key Excerpts

Surfaces topical themes from your documents by grouping related passages. A starting point for getting oriented in a large case — not every excerpt will be a hit, but the good ones save real time.

Who Is This For?

  • Anyone wary of sending documents to an AI cloud

How It Works

CasePrepd is a desktop app, not a web service. You load your documents (PDF, Word, text files, and even scanned images), and everything is processed locally using built-in AI. No internet connection is needed at any point — not during installation, not during use. The app never connects to the internet, period.

Under the hood, CasePrepd uses multiple on-device AI models for search, name recognition, and vocabulary filtering. Everything ships with the installer — nothing is downloaded at runtime.

FAQ

Why is this free?

I'm a court reporter who built this for my own use and wanted to share it with the community. It's open source and always will be.

Is my data really private?

Yes. CasePrepd never connects to the internet. All processing happens on your machine using models that ship with the installer. No data is uploaded, logged, or transmitted anywhere.

Do I need a powerful computer?

You need 16 GB of RAM and about 4 GB of disk space. CasePrepd runs entirely on CPU — no GPU required.

What file types are supported?

PDF, Word (.docx), plain text (.txt), and images. Scanned PDFs are automatically read using built-in text recognition (OCR) — no extra software needed.
